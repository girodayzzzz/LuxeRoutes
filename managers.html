What managers do

Managers support LuxeRoutes by identifying exceptional stays, contacting suitable owners, and building trusted local networks in their country or niche.

  • Find luxury stays
  • Contact property owners
  • Onboard partners
  • Build regional networks
  • Support growth in their country or niche

Earnings concept

Performance-based opportunity

LuxeRoutes is developing a commission-based manager model connected to approved partners, active campaigns, and future subscriptions or bookings.

Commission opportunities may depend on approved partnerships, active campaigns, and future monetization models.

No guaranteed income is promised. The program is intended for people who can build quality relationships and support premium growth over time.
