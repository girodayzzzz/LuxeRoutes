Cookie Policy

Starter cookie information for the LuxeRoutes static website.

LuxeRoutes is currently a lightweight static website. The site is designed to avoid unnecessary tracking and heavy third-party scripts during early launch.

Essential functionality

Basic website functionality, such as the mobile navigation menu, runs in the browser and does not require a user account, booking engine, or payment flow.

Future tools

If LuxeRoutes later adds analytics, embedded forms, advertising pixels, or preference tools, this Cookie Policy will be updated to explain what is used and why.

Inquiries and listings

Inquiries are not confirmed bookings. Listings may be curated or illustrative during early launch, and final terms are confirmed with the provider or LuxeRoutes team.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Questions can be sent to hello@luxeroutes.eu.
