## Route summary

Begin with Alpine lake calm, continue through wine country, and finish with a private Adriatic villa or island stay.

## Suggested flow

1. Slovenia lake arrival
2. Brda or Istria wine country
3. Croatian coast finale

## Experience ideas

- Private driver between bases
- Wine tasting or vineyard lunch
- Yacht day or island lunch
- Chef dinner at the final villa

## Stay pairing ideas

- Lake villa or boutique hotel
- Wine country retreat
- Adriatic private villa
