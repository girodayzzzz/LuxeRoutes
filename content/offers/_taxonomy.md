# LuxeRoutes offer taxonomy

Uporabljaj spodnje vrednosti za filtre, front matter in prihodnje generatorje kartic. Vrednosti so zapisane kot `slug`, ker se ujemajo z obstoječimi HTML `data-*` atributi.

## Countries

- `slovenia` — Slovenia
- `croatia` — Croatia
- `italy` — Italy
- `austria` — Austria
- `switzerland` — Switzerland
- `france` — France

## Regions

- `alps` — Alps
- `adriatic` — Adriatic coast
- `lakes` — Lakes
- `wine-country` — Wine country
- `city` — City
- `countryside` — Countryside
- `riviera` — Riviera

## Accommodation types

- `villa` — Private villa
- `chalet` — Chalet
- `boutique-hotel` — Boutique hotel
- `apartment` — Apartment
- `cabin` — Cabin
- `retreat` — Wellness retreat

## Options

- `pool` — Pool
- `spa` — Spa or sauna
- `sea-view` — Sea view
- `family` — Family ready
- `pet-friendly` — Pet friendly
- `private-chef` — Private chef option

## Trip types

- `signature-route` — Signature route
- `romantic-getaway` — Romantic getaway
- `wine-tour` — Wine tour
- `wellness-retreat` — Wellness retreat
- `yacht-experience` — Yacht experience
- `fishing-escape` — Fishing escape

## Naming checklist

- `title` naj bo lep prikazni naslov.
- `slug` naj bo enak imenu datoteke brez `.md`.
- `country`, `region`, `type` in `options` naj uporabljajo samo vrednosti iz te datoteke.
- Če ponudba ni pripravljena za javno objavo, nastavi `status: draft`.
