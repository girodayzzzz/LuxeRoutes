Private brief FAQ

What happens after you send the brief?

Use the form when you have a direction, a stay request, or just a travel mood. LuxeRoutes replies with the next most useful step.

Do I need exact dates before sending an inquiry?

No. Exact dates help, but a flexible range is enough to start shaping the right route and stay direction.

Is this an instant booking website?

No. LuxeRoutes is inquiry-led: availability, inclusions, provider terms, and final pricing are confirmed only after the brief is reviewed.

Can you combine stays, transfers, and experiences?

Yes. The strongest requests usually combine route flow, curated stays, private transfers, and a few experiences that fit the occasion.
