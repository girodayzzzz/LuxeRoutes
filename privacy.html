Privacy Policy

Starter privacy information for an early-stage EU luxury travel platform.

LuxeRoutes is an early-stage European luxury travel platform. This Privacy Policy explains how information submitted through the website may be used to respond to inquiries and partnership conversations.

Information you provide

If you submit a contact, partner, or manager form, we may receive your name, email address, inquiry type, message, location details, and any information you choose to include.

How information may be used

Data submitted through forms may be used to respond to inquiries, review partner fit, discuss manager opportunities, and improve the early LuxeRoutes service concept.

Bookings and providers

Inquiries are not confirmed bookings. Listings may be curated or illustrative during early launch. Final terms, availability, and any service details are confirmed with the relevant provider or the LuxeRoutes team.

Cookies and analytics

The current static website is designed to stay lightweight. If analytics or optional cookies are introduced later, this policy and the Cookie Policy will be updated.

Your choices

You may ask us to review or delete information you submitted by contacting hello@luxeroutes.eu.
