How it works

What happens after you submit your private brief.

Your message is a private inquiry, not a booking commitment. LuxeRoutes reviews the details personally, checks fit and availability, then replies with a clear proposal direction before anything is reserved.

01

Brief received privately

We read your dates, group size, preferred regions, budget guidance, and non-negotiables so the first reply is focused.

02

Availability is checked

Stays, transfers, and experiences are treated as proposal targets until availability and pricing are confirmed personally.

03

You decide next steps

We reply with questions or a first direction. There is no obligation to proceed until you approve the scope in writing.
